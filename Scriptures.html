    • John 5:37-39
      37 And the Father himself, which hath sent me, hath borne witness of me.
      Ye have neither heard his voice at any time, nor seen his shape.
      38 And ye have not his word abiding in you: for whom he hath sent, him ye believe not.
      39 Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me.

    Jesus, after healing a lame man, was being accused by Jews in the temple at Jerusalem for healing a man on the sabbath. He replies, "My Father worketh hitherto, and I work. "(v.17)
    The Jews then seek to kill him, not only for healing on the sabbath. But now he's making himself out to be equal to God?! Jesus is about to tell them ,in so many words, who he is: (Deuteronomy 19:15 One witness shall not rise up against a man for any iniquity, or for any sin, in any sin that he sinneth: at the mouth of two witnesses, or at the mouth of three witnesses, shall the matter be established.)

    Jesus isnt just making some bold, outlandish statement. He's got witnesses. He mentions John the Baptist (v32-33), and then he mentions the Father himself, which sent him (v.37). He goes on to say,
    "You dont have the Father's word in you, because you don't believe the one he sent...
    Search the scriptures [Old Testament], thats how you find out how to get eternal life, and they also testify of me"

    The Lord then stated he'll put enmity (opposition) betewwn the serpent's (Satan) seed and the woman's seed... but we know, biologically, that women don't carry the seed.
    This is the first prophecy concerning Jesus being born of a virgin.
